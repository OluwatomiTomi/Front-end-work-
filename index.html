ABOUT PROJECT

In every true democratic country, the security of an election is a matter of national security interest because this a reflection of the popular consensus of its citizens therefore this creates an incentive for development, growth and innovation to combat against any form of foul play orchestrated by a bad actor, this level of election security is a concern on various levels on a macro scale such as a country to a semi-micro scale such as an educational institution or a Work Organization/Company. From the early stages of democratically electing candidates, the voting process has primarily been based on the pen & paper balloting system, any fault proof plan of substituting the traditional pen & paper scheme with a new electoral system is vital to limit/eradicate fraudulent activities and schemes plot by bad actors on both a Micro & Marco scale ensuring a developing voting process which promotes the notion/practice of traceability, Transparency and verifiability.

Blockchain is a competent tool for conducting a successful Electoral vote. Blockchain is a distributed digital ledger of cryptographically signed transactions which are grouped into blocks. Each block is fundamentally linked (cryptographically) to the previous one (making it tamper evident) after validation and undergoing a consensus decision.
